Length of Exposed Critical Traces

Introduction

All critical nets must be buried between solid ground/power planes. Portions of critical nets on outside layers must be kept shorter than 5.08 mm. The total exposed length of a critical net on an outside layer must be kept to less than 12.7 mm. If no buried wiring layers exist, critical traces must be run on the side of card (top or bottom) nearest the grounded case.
